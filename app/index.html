PHL Crime Mapper

This application shows you the Part I (serious) crimes around you in the last month.

To begin, select a radius around your current location that you want to display crimes for.


Philly Crime Mapper

This application allows you to draw an area and view the Part I (serious) crimes in that region over the last three years.

To begin, select a time period. Then, click the draw tool above to draw the area you are interested in.
